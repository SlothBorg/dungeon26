## Landmark

### Storage room

A small stairwell and is tucked away into the corner of the main hall, down it's creaking wooden stairs is a dingy basement full of large barrels and boxes. All the goods and food supplies are kept here, due to it subterranean nature it's frequently infested by burrowing creatures. In the far end of the room a large metal frame stands draped in an oilcloth, a vein attempt to prevent rust. 

## Hidden

The frame is in fact a one way portal that is used to smuggle in goods. 

## Secret

While it is true that burrowing creatures will sometimes find there way into the storage room. Lehtinen makes no great effort to prevent it as it offers a great cover for when he's had too much to drink and loses control of his shape.
