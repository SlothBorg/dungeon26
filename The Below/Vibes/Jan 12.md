## The Library

The lair of a powerful lich obsessed with knowledge and secrets, largely harmless less it's beloved library is disturbed.

### Sights & Structures

Corridors and rooms overflowing with crumbling books, piles of dusty scrolls, desks with skeletons slumped at them. 

### Sounds

Any sound above a whisper is met with shushes from unseen librarians. 

### Smell and Taste

Dust and old books. 


### Lighting

Fire is strictly forbidden, it's sight calls to the ancient guardians of the place. 


### Common Monsters

Skeletons, desiccated husks, and mummies wander the halls. Brave or foolish scholars sneak from room to room searching for forgotten knowledge.
