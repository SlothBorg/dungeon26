## Landmark

The corridor depends a flight of stairs onto a open circular room dominated by a large round desk. Piled high with stacks of books.

In the middle of the desk a hunched figure snores softly.

A faded sign hangs from cobweb covered chains, it reads "Information" in countless languages.

## Hidden

The librarian is gruff but with prompting will answer questions.

## Secret

The librarian knows the Alicia name, and how it can be used to navigate the library but will only divulge as much if directly and specifically asked
