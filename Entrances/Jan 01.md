## Landmark

The chamber is *dank* with a low ceiling, and moss cling clings to the *flagstone*.

In the center a rusted *iron grate* covers a low *well*, now run dry.

A *rusted spear* has been used to bar the grate shut, from the spear dangles a *skeleton* the spear seems to have replaces the skeleton's spine!

## Hidden

### Rumor

* Before his death in **The Below**, the adventurer **Krostrak Bluntborn** spoke of a **forbidden well** dug deep into the stone. The waters within are said to flow from the **River of Death**.
